The resolution of STED and multiphoton microscopes depends critically on the shape of the focal spot — the point-spread function (PSF) — which degrades with depth because of optical aberrations. I worked on the vectorial simulation of the PSF and on aberration correction to recover resolution deep inside tissue.

This led to **FASER**, an open-source tool to simulate realistic PSF distortions in STED microscopy, developed with J. Roos and U. V. Nägerl.
