Standard SHG reveals where collagen is, but not the direction in which the fibrils point. Interferometric SHG (I-SHG) measures the *phase* of the second-harmonic field, giving access to the absolute polarity and orientation of non-centrosymmetric assemblies.

Developed during my postdoc with F. Légaré (INRS-EMT), I used I-SHG — including fast, high-contrast and Gouy-phase-shift implementations — to probe the polarity of collagen fibrils in connective tissues and of microtubules in mitotic spindles.
