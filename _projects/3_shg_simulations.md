SHG images are shaped not only by the amount of collagen present, but by how fibrils are arranged and oriented at the nanoscale. I develop numerical models of the SHG process in collagen — including the coherent build-up of the signal, the forward/backward emission ratio and the effect of refractive-index mismatch — to interpret experimental images quantitatively.

These simulations make it possible to extract physical parameters that are otherwise inaccessible, such as collagen fibril diameter and polarity, directly from measured SHG signals.
